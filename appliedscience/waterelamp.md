## ðŸ›  Step-by-Step Instructions

### **1. Prepare the Cup**

* Take a food delivery package waste like small sauce package (best option) and clean it.

### **2. Insert Wires**

* Strip the ends of two wires (remove insulation).
* Insert the bare ends inside the two holes.

These will touch the saltwater later.

### **3. Connect the Transistor**

* Connect **one wire** to the **base** of the transistor.

Where:

* C = Collector
* B = Base
* E = Emitter

### **4. Connect the LED**

* Connect the **positive leg (longer leg)** of the LED to the **positive terminal** of the coin cell.
* Connect the **negative leg** of the LED to the **collector** of the transistor.

### **5. Connect Battery Negative**

* Connect the **negative terminal** of the battery to the **emitter** of the transistor.

### **6. Add Saltwater**

* Put **1 spoon of salt** in the cup.
* Pour water inside.

As soon as water touches the two wire ends â†’
**small current â†’ transistor ON â†’ LED glows!**
